Buñuelosh



You can find many versions of buñuelos. The Mexican version are sweet, sometimes round and plump and other times flat, like an American elephant ear. They're often dusted in sugar or a piloncillo syrup, but you can also enjoy them plain. Try them as a sweet dessert or even a special breakfast.
